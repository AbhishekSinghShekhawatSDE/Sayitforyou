Why We Exist

This isn't just a service; it's a safe space born from understanding the weight of unspoken words.

We started "Say It For You" as a social experiment to bridge the gap created by shyness, social anxiety, or emotional blocks. It's for anyone needing to:

  • Confess feelings honestly.
  • Offer a sincere apology.
  • Express hidden gratitude.
  • Provide or seek closure.

Your anonymity is our priority. Your message, delivered with care.

Send Your Message Anonymously

1. Fill the Form

Write your message, give the Instagram handle of the person you want to send it to, and choose the message type — Text, Voice, or Video.

2. Pay via UPI

Scan the QR code shown in the form and pay the small fee — ₹5 for Text, ₹15 for Voice, ₹30 for Video — using any UPI app.

3. Add Txn Ref ID

After paying, enter your UPI transaction ID or upload a screenshot in the form. This helps us confirm your payment

4. We Verify & Deliver

Once we confirm the payment, we’ll send your message to the person — anonymously or with your name (your choice). You’ll get a message once it’s delivered.
